    Where We Started

    Alfredo, Nori, JM, and KL first met each other inside the tiny hot room of PUP Main's 4th floor East wing. It took them a while before they actually became friends and from then on, they navigated college life together, through thick and thin.

    After being separated during their sophomore year, the four reunited and became classmates again on their junior year. Billing Management System

Billing Management System

Admin User Interface

This billing management system platform allows GS-Tech's administrators to automatically send billing statements to customers every month, resolve a ticket, tag payments and prorate discounts, get an overview of their sales, and more.

This ticketing management system allows PUP SIS Administrators to keep track of various requests from students and professors regarding their SIS accounts. These includes enrollment, grades, and other general concerns.
